Who We Are

BMC
Business And Management Club

We are the Business and Management Club of IIITDM Jabalpur, a student driven community dedicated to building leadership, communication and entrepreneurial thinking among our students.

Our club bridges the gap between classroom learning and real-world professional experience through workshops, expert talks, case studies, and competitions. We aim to create a space where ideas grow, skills sharpen and students gain the confidence to solve problems, lead teams and make an impact.
